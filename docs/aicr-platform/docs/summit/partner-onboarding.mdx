# Partner Onboarding

Partners are third-party organizations that extend the AICR platform by publishing packs to the marketplace or deploying AICR as a white-label platform for their clients.

## Partner Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Pack Author** | Builds and publishes packs to the AICR marketplace |
| **Enterprise Client** | Uses AICR as a white-label platform for their own clients |
| **Integration Partner** | Connects external services to AICR via the INCC |
| **Reseller** | Sells AICR deployments to end customers |

## Onboarding Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply for partner status">
    Submit a partner application at bluehorizonsgroup.com/partners. Include your organization details, intended pack categories, and technical capabilities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agreement and review">
    Sign the partner agreement. BHG reviews and approves within 5 business days. The TenantAgent creates your partner tenant.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Development access">
    Receive access to the AICR development environment, partner documentation, and the Pack Authoring SDK. A dedicated KnowledgeAgent context is set up for your organization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build and certify">
    Develop your pack(s) in Studio. Submit for BHG certification review. The MarketplaceAgent coordinates the review process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Marketplace listing">
    Approved packs are listed in the marketplace with your verified partner badge. Revenue share begins on first tenant activation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Partner Requirements

- Must pass SecurityAuditor review for all packs
- Must maintain documentation for all published surfaces
- Must respond to P0 issues within 24 hours
- Must comply with AICR's data handling policies

## Revenue Share

Partners receive a percentage of pack activation fees based on:
- Pack category and tier
- Number of active tenants
- Partnership tier (standard, preferred, strategic)

Contact partners@bluehorizonsgroup.com for revenue share details.
